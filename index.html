Iceland

by Meagan Hiatt, travelled May 16th - June 7th, 2016

 

Earlier this summer I went to Iceland for three weeks, with my two roomates (Michael and Kevin). We backpacked and camped all around the southwestern part of Iceland. These webpages show our journey as well as some things we learned (about Iceland as well as backpacking in general.

 

Here are most of the places we visited! We travelled by either walking or taking the bus and carried our backpacks and sleeping bags.
